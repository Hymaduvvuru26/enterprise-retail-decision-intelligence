| KPI                   | Formula                            | Business Value      |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------- |
| Total Revenue         | Sum(payment_value)                 | Overall sales       |
| Total Orders          | Count(order_id)                    | Business volume     |
| Average Order Value   | Revenue / Orders                   | Spending behavior   |
| Repeat Purchase Rate  | Repeat Customers / Total Customers | Loyalty             |
| Average Delivery Time | Delivery Date - Purchase Date      | Logistics           |
| Late Delivery %       | Late Deliveries / Total Deliveries | Service quality     |
| Customer Satisfaction | Average Review Score               | Customer experience |
