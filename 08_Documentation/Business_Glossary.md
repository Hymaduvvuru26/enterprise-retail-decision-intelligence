| Term                    | Meaning                                        |
| ----------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Customer Lifetime Value | Total revenue generated by a customer          |
| Average Order Value     | Revenue divided by number of orders            |
| Delivery Delay          | Actual delivery date - Estimated delivery date |
| Churn                   | Customer stops purchasing                      |
| Gross Revenue           | Total payment amount before deductions         |
| Repeat Customer         | Customer with more than one completed order    |
